What automation actually frees up

The hours your team
spends on repetitive work.

Most businesses have at least a handful of processes that follow the same pattern every time: collect this data, move it here, send this email, update this spreadsheet. Each one might only take 10 minutes. But across a team, across a week, that adds up to hours of time that could be spent on work that actually requires human judgement.

We identify the highest-value automation opportunities in your business, build the systems and make sure they run reliably. Not every process should be automated — some are too variable, some are too important to get wrong. We'll tell you which is which.

We work with your existing tools wherever possible — Zapier, Make, HubSpot, your email platform. We build custom systems when the off-the-shelf options genuinely don't fit. The result is always the same: reliable, documented automation that your team can understand and maintain.

Every automation we build includes error handling, logging and alerts. If something breaks, you know immediately. We don't build and disappear.

How we work
  • 01
    Workflow audit

    We map your current processes, identify the repetitive patterns and score each opportunity by time saving, complexity and risk. You get a prioritised list of what to automate and in what order.

  • 02
    Design and build

    We design each automation to be reliable and maintainable — with proper error handling, logging and documentation from the start.

  • 03
    Test and stabilise

    Thorough testing before go-live, followed by a stabilisation period where we monitor closely and fix anything that surfaces in real conditions.

  • 04
    Handover

    Full documentation and training so your team can manage the automations independently. We explain how each one works, what to watch for and what to do if something goes wrong.

Before you automate
a business process.

We start with a workflow audit — mapping what your team does, how long it takes and where the bottlenecks are. We then score opportunities by potential time saving, implementation complexity and reliability risk. Not everything should be automated, and we'll tell you when something isn't worth it.

For most workflow automations, the breakeven is 3–6 months. Some simpler automations — like automated reporting or email triage — pay back in weeks. We'll give you an honest ROI estimate before starting any project.

We build in error handling, logging and alerts so if something goes wrong, you know about it immediately and can act. We don't build and disappear — we include a stabilisation period after launch.

We work with your existing tools wherever possible — Zapier, Make, HubSpot, Salesforce, your CRM, your email platform. We only build custom systems when off-the-shelf solutions genuinely don't fit.
