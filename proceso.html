The process

Four phases.
Zero surprises.

Phase 01

Discovery & Audit

Free 30-minute strategy call. We ask the right questions to understand your business, stack and objectives.

  • Business & goal alignment
  • Current tech stack audit
  • Competitor benchmarking
  • Quick-win identification
Duration: 1–3 days
Phase 02

Proposal & Agreement

Fixed-price proposal with defined deliverables, realistic timeline and KPIs we will be held to.

  • Fixed-price quote
  • Defined deliverables
  • Milestone timeline
  • KPI & success criteria
Duration: 2–5 days
Phase 03

Agile Execution

Start within 48 hours of sign-off. Two-week sprints with a demo or delivery at the end of every sprint.

  • Two-week sprint cycles
  • Weekly status updates
  • Slack channel access
  • Mid-point review call
Duration: 2–8 weeks
Phase 04

Handover & Growth

Full documentation, team training and clean handover. Optional retainer for continued optimisation.

  • Full technical documentation
  • Team training sessions
  • 30-day bug fix guarantee
  • Optional growth retainer
Duration: 1 week
