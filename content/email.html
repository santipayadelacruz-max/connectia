Email is the channel most businesses underinvest in relative to its actual return — partly because it doesn't feel as exciting as a new ad campaign, partly because a bad sequence is invisible until you check the numbers and realise nobody's opening anything.

We build sequences around the specific moment someone's in — a brand-new subscriber needs a different message than someone who's browsed three times without buying. Generic "newsletter" content sent to everyone the same way is the single biggest waste of email's potential we see.
