The technical foundations

Great content on a technically broken site won't rank. We fix the infrastructure issues that prevent search engines from properly crawling, understanding and ranking your pages.

Let's talk → ← SEO

Technical SEO is invisible work — when it's done right, nobody notices. When it's done wrong, your content can be perfect and still not rank, because Google simply can't crawl, render or understand your pages properly.

We see the same patterns repeatedly: sites built on platforms that weren't designed with SEO in mind, migrations that broke redirects, JavaScript-heavy pages that don't render properly for crawlers, and international sites with hreflang configured incorrectly from the start.

Fixing these isn't glamorous, but it's usually the highest-leverage work available. A site that loads in 1.5 seconds instead of 4 seconds, with clean crawlable architecture, gives every piece of content you publish a better chance of ranking — including content you've already written.
