There's no Search Console equivalent for AI visibility yet — no single dashboard that tells you definitively how often your brand appears across ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini. So we built our own structured approach: a consistent set of queries relevant to your business, run at regular intervals, with results logged and compared over time.

It's more manual than traditional rank tracking, and it should be treated as directional rather than perfectly precise — AI responses vary between sessions even for identical queries. But run consistently over months, the pattern becomes clear: are you appearing more or less often, in what context, alongside which competitors.
