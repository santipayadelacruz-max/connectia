GEO Strategy

A GEO strategy built
for your brand.

GEO isn't SEO with a different name. It requires understanding how AI engines source answers, what signals they trust and how to position your brand as the authoritative answer.

Let's talk → ← GEO Optimization

Nobody has GEO fully figured out yet — including us. What we have is a working theory backed by what we're seeing in practice: AI engines cite sources that demonstrate clear expertise, structure information for easy extraction, and have enough independent authority signals that the AI "trusts" the source.

Our approach starts with finding out where you currently stand. We run a structured set of queries relevant to your business across ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini and record what comes back — does your brand appear, who appears instead, and why might that be.

From there it's a roadmap, not a single fix. Content restructured for clarity, entity signals strengthened, authority built through genuine mentions and citations elsewhere. It overlaps heavily with good SEO — which is partly why businesses already doing SEO well tend to have a head start in GEO too.

What we know is that AI engines cite brands with strong authority signals, well-structured content and clear entity presence — the same foundations as good SEO, with additional specific work. Featured snippets appear in Google results — part of traditional SEO. GEO is about appearing in AI tool responses (ChatGPT, Perplexity) which have their own sourcing logic. There's overlap but GEO needs additional entity and citation-building work.
